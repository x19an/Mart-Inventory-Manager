## ðŸ’¾ Data Safety & Backups
This app stores data locally in your browser. 
- **DO NOT** clear your browser's "Site Data" or "Cache" for localhost, or you will lose your inventory records.
- **ALWAYS** go to the **Settings** tab and click **"Download .db File"** at the end of every business day.
- To move your data to a new computer, simply install the app there and use the **"Import .db File"** feature.
